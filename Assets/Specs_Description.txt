Buyers can choose between RWD or 4x4, with the 2.8L delivering quicker 0-100 km/h times (~10-11 seconds). Fuel economy ranges from 10-14 km/L, competitive for its class. Inside, it features a spacious ladder-frame SUV cabin, Toyota Safety Sense, leather seats (higher trims), and touchscreen infotainment. Priced between ?1.7M-?2.5M (higher for LTD and GR Sport), it remains a top choice in the PH SUV market with Toyota's extensive service network and excellent long-term value.
